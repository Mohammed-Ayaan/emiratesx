Landmarks Detected by EX Vision

EX Vision currently identifies the following iconic UAE landmarks. More locations may be added in the future as the model evolves:

Burj Khalifa

Located in Dubai, the Burj Khalifa is the tallest structure in the world, standing at over 828 meters. It’s a global symbol of innovation and engineering excellence.

Burj Al Arab

This luxury hotel, also in Dubai, is known for its sail-shaped silhouette and is one of the most recognizable hotel buildings in the world.

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

Located in Abu Dhabi, this mosque is one of the largest in the world. It is renowned for its stunning white marble, intricate design, and cultural significance.

Custom Image Dataset

EX Vision is powered by a dataset built from scratch using curated images of UAE landmarks, ensuring tailored accuracy and performance.

Efficient Deep Learning

Using a lightweight convolutional neural network optimized for real-time classification in the browser, EX Vision delivers fast and accurate results without extra installations.

Showcasing UAE Icons

Designed to promote cultural awareness and technological innovation, EX Vision celebrates the architectural wonders of the United Arab Emirates.

EmiratesX

This is a UAE-focused educational and eco-awareness app developed by Mohammed Ayaan for the ADNOC competition. It features Arabic language learning, daily AI-generated eco tips, a quiz-based leaderboard, a smart EX Store with 19 products across 4 categories, and informative sections on UAE’s culture, arts, festivals, and history. The app supports light/dark modes, user profiles, and secure sign-in, with an AI assistant that answers all app-related questions.
